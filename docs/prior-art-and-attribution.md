# Prior Art and Attribution — x402-Ethereum

**Author:** Richard Patterson (@De-ASI-INTERFACE) | **Date:** 2026-07-09

---

The x402-Ethereum Extension was originated and authored by Richard Patterson as part of the x402 multi-chain payment routing protocol family. It extends the canonical x402-Solana specification (De-ASI-INTERFACE/x402-solana-extension) to Ethereum's EVM environment.

Prior art consulted: EIP-712 (typed data signing), EIP-2612 (permit), EIP-1559 (fee market). None define an HTTP-layer payment gating protocol. This binding is the original work of Richard Patterson.
