## Relationship to x402 Standard

The x402 open standard was published at https://x402.org. This extension adapts that standard specifically to Ethereum's EIP-712, ERC-20, and Uniswap v4 architecture. All Ethereum-specific design decisions are original work by Richard Patterson.
